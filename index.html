Experience the Magic of the Northern Lights

Science of Phenomena

The Northern Lights, or aurora borealis, form when charged particles from the sun interact with Earth's atmosphere, creating stunning displays of colorful light in the polar regions.

Unique Experiences

Here are some unique experiences related to the Northern Lights: Dog Sledding under the Aurora: Traverse the snowy landscapes of the Arctic while being guided by a team of huskies, all while the Northern Lights dance overhead.
